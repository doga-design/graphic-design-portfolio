D&Y Pilates Studio Website

This project is a full redesign of my sister's real Pilates studio website. While her physical studio reflects her personality and values—calm, strength, and mindfulness—the original website did the opposite. It used a loud, aggressive gym template that felt completely disconnected from her brand and actually pushed potential clients away.

Pilates isn't about intensity for intensity's sake. It's about thoughtful movement, body awareness, and creating space to feel strong and balanced. The original site's visuals and user experience didn't communicate any of that. It was cluttered, overwhelming, and even had random filler blog posts that didn't serve any purpose.

Role
UI, visual design
Focus
Homepage, booking flow, responsive layout
← back to all work

I redesigned the site to feel clean, modern, and calming—removing distractions and focusing on what really matters: her expertise, the benefits of Pilates, and making it easy for clients to book or learn more. The result is a digital presence that finally matches the real-world experience of her studio.

Boots & Hearts Festival Branding

This project is a full branding and digital design concept for Boots and Hearts, Canada's biggest country music festival. The goal was to create a cohesive visual identity that captures the spirit of the event—celebrating community, music, and the country lifestyle—while also encouraging ticket sales and online engagement.

The original focus was on building a sense of warmth, fun, and authenticity through color, typography, and layout. I used bittersweet peach and soft yellow as brand colors to keep the vibe vibrant and welcoming, while rustic textures and iconography helped tie everything back to the country aesthetic.

Role
Brand design, digital design

Brand kit, poster series, web & app mockups
